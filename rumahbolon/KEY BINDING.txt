############# Keyboard Control #############

A	: Strafe Left
D	: Strafe Right
W	: Pane Up
S	: Pane Down
E	: Toggle Depth Test (On / Off)
1	: Toggle Animate Rotation (On / Off)
Q	: Quit

############# Mouse Control #############

Left Click + Mouse Move	: Camera
Right Click		: View Interior
Middle Click		: Toggle Lighting (On / Off)
Scroll Up		: Zoom In
Scroll Down		: Zoom Out
